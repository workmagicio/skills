## Output template

One message, 3 parts: **anchor → question + options → default + escape hatch**.

<callout emoji="💬">
Got it — you want to know where to cut spend. To answer that, I need to know the granularity:
1. By **channel** (which platform to pull back from)
2. By **campaign** (which specific campaigns are underperforming)
3. By **creative** (which ad creatives are dragging ROAS down)
If you don't say, I'll go with **by campaign** — that's usually the most actionable level.
</callout>

### Rules for the template

- Anchor goes first. Even one word ("Got it — Meta, last 30 days.") earns trust
- Question = single sentence; **not** "Could you tell me a few things?" — that signals stacking
- Options must be 2–4 concrete + mutually exclusive interpretations
- Each option has a short clarifier in parens — business outcome / what user can DO with that view
- Default is bolded; explanation in 1 clause why ("usually the most actionable")
- End with a clear escape hatch — "tell me 1 / 2 / 3 or 'just pick'"
